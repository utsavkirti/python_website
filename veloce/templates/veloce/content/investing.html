Beagle is an online market place where the investor gets to play the bank and collect EMIs each month as the borrower is paying off the debt.

Here on Beagle the platform is provided to the credit worthy borrowers who has passed the scrutiny and CIBIL score of the team of Beagle and willing investors transact for short or long terms capital investment. Beagle provides a tailor made portfolios to the lenders where they can diversify their funds into various profiles and earn high interest rates on their invested capital.

At Beagle the Investor is provided with a feature on our website which is called Portfolio Manager where the investor can set a desired risk tolerance bar and tick off the bid sizes to invest in loan settings with that the lenders get a related list of borrowers along with all the details.

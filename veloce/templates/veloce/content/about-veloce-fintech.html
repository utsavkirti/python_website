www.beaglefintech.com is an associated finance facility portal with www.beagle.bazar Whereas, www.beagle.bazar is medical technology & equipment [meditech] marketplace which aims to make the latest medical equipment and technologies available to the doctors, hospitals and health care providers. The platform provides a facility for medical equipment manufacturers and dealers to display and list their latest products and thereby reach out to millions of doctors, hospitals and healthcare providers across the country.

Given that the doctors may find it difficult to self-finance some of the costlier equipment and machines, Beagle also provides a finance platform www.beaglefintech.com wherein approved dealers can offer various financing solutions to the doctors by way of easy and low interest EMI loans.

Beagle will provide an online platform where the lender and borrower get variety of options for interest rates and returns. The actual lending and borrowing would be done directly between the lender and the borrower offline in a transparent manner i.e. not through the bank accounts of the Company but directly between the lender and borrower.

At Beagle lending is as safe as it can be if you use trusted platforms. An investment made under such platform provides higher returns on the capital. Only risk is the risk of default made by the borrower which is risk of lending in any other finances. However Beagle ensures risk evaluation by its team with certain practices.
